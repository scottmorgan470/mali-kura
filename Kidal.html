Region Kidal

Kidal Region the eigth administrative region of Mali, covering 151,450 square kilometres or 58,480 square miles. This area was formerly part of Gao Region, it lies 285 km (177 mi) northeast of Gao but was created as a separate region in 1991. Kidal is mainly Tuareg, it’s a town and commune in the desert region of northern Mali. It has been invaded by a rebel group called National Movement of Liberation of Azawad (MNLA) after the overthrown of Libya Gaddafi; The MNLA proclaimed to be the independent nation-state of Azawad on 6 April 2012. This independence has not been recognized by any country or international organization. It was under the control of different armed movements such as the Ansar Dine and MNLA until January 2013. The Region's administrative capital is the town of Kidal. . The commune has an area of about 9,910 km2 (3,830 sq mi) and includes the town of Kidal and 31 other settlements
